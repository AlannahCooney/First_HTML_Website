Hecate was the goddess of magic, witchcraft, the night, moon, ghosts and necromancy in Greek Mythology.She was the child of the Titanes Perses and Asteria from whom she received her power over heaven, earth and sea.

Hecate assisted Demeter in her search for Persephone, guiding her through the night with flaming torches. After the mother-daughter reunion , she became Persephone's minister and companion in Haides.
